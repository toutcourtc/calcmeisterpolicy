Privacy Policy of CalcMeister

This privacy policy outlines the types of information that the app may collect from its users and the practices for collecting, using, storing, and protecting those information.

Collection and Use of Information

The CalcMeister app does not collect any personally identifiable data from its users. It does not request or collect names, email addresses, telephone numbers, or other information that could personally identify you.

However, the app does store user-configured settings to facilitate navigation upon reopening the app.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The app does not use cookies, web beacons, or other similar technologies to collect information about users.

Sharing of Information

The app does not share any information about its users with third parties, for commercial purposes or otherwise.

Children

CalcMeister is designed for use by people of all ages, including children. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove that information from our systems.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes to this policy will be posted here.
